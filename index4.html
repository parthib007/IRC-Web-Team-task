INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS CELL

The International Relations Cell (IRC) is a student body working along with the Office of International Relations (OIR). It works to promote the exchange of students, faculties, and ideas between IIT Kharagpur and her international partners and establishing the globalization of IIT Kharagpur.
